My Sports life

Over my lifetime I have played many sports. When I was very young I started with soccer, backetball and baseball. At the time I loved all three of these sports, but as time went on things got more competitive. I was more of the "I just want to have fun" kind of kid. So I stopped playing basketball and baseball. I continued playing soccer until my freshman year of high school. The reason I stopped is so I could focus on school and focus all my athletic abilities on my new favorite sport, tennis. Tennis has been a big part of my life both in school and out. It helped me make some good friends and connections I never would have made without it.

Sports has always played a big role in my life and will always continue to. I try and get out to play any sport as often as I can just to try and stay active and get some sun outside.
